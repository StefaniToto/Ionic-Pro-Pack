Text to Speech

There are two plugins for text to speech functionality - Capacitor ScreenReader and Text to Speech plugin

Capacitor ScreenReader plugin works with VoiceOver (iOS) and TalkBack (Android), which are accessibility features for people who cannot easily read text in apps. You can enable these features in your device from Settings.

Text to Speech plugin can work without turning ON the VoiceOver (iOS) or TalkBack (Android)
